# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-07

Dinosaur Eats does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## What the extension accesses

- The extension runs a content script on pages you visit to animate and modify visible page text.
- The extension stores one local preference in your browser (`dinoEatsEnabled`) so ON/OFF state persists.

## What the extension does not do

- No analytics
- No tracking
- No remote API calls
- No sale or sharing of personal data

## Data storage

- Storage location: `chrome.storage.local` on your device
- Data stored: enabled/disabled toggle only
- Data retention: until you remove the extension or clear extension storage
